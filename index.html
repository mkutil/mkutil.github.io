The Impact of Virtualisation on small to medium-sized businesses


About me

My name is Miroslav Kutil and I am student of Computer Network Engineering with Administration at The Manchester College. WRITE MORE!




Why this research?

The reason this topic has been chosen is that the virtualization is step forward in technology as we know it. According to Yeger (2004) one of main the main benefits of the virtual architecture is the ability to control and manage tasks of different network technologies by the same controller. One physical machine can host many Virtual Machines with different operating systems running different applications.

Justification of the Research

According to Opara and Soluade (2013) small and mid-size businesses can hugely benefit from saving resources, increasing system stability when shorten downtime and improved security. My personal interest in this topic is purely for future employment opportunities. Virtualization has much to offer and when implemented properly it can save money and improve overall performance of a business.
